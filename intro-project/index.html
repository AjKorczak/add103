This year is my first year at MCC. One of my biggest passions is photography. I love capturing moments and turning them into art. I enjoy finding beauty in everyday life. I often carry my camera with me, ready to snap a photo whenever inspiration strikes. Photography allows me to express my thoughts and feelings.

I also enjoy creating digital art. With the help of graphic design software, I can blend colors, shapes, and images to bring my ideas to life. I love experimenting with different styles, whether it's designing colorful posters or creating illustrations for fun. I often find myself lost in the creative process for hours.

As I work toward my goal of becoming a graphic designer after college, these hobbies play a crucial role in making sure not only do I not burn out, as well as, helping me build my skills and develop my artistic voice. I am excited to learn more about graphic design at MCC and hope to use my photography and digital art to create amazing projects.
