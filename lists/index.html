Defintions

Red
a color at the end of the spectrum next to orange and opposite violet.
Orange
reddish yellow in color.
Yellow
the color between green and orange in the spectrum, a primary subtractive color complementary to blue.
Green
the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum.
Blue
a color intermediate between green and violet, as of the sky or sea on a sunny day.
Purple
a color intermediate between red and blue.
